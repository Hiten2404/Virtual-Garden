Thyme (Thymus vulgaris)

Thyme is a fragrant herb native to the Mediterranean region, often used in cooking, medicine, and aromatherapy. It is a hardy perennial known for its small, aromatic leaves.
Plant Attributes & Uses

Scientific Classification

Family: Lamiaceae
Genus: Thymus
Species: T. vulgaris

Global Spread

Native to Mediterranean regions, now cultivated worldwide.

Preferred Climate

Warm, dry climates with well-drained soil.

Medicinal Uses

Used to treat coughs, sore throats, and digestive issues.

Traditional Uses

Used in herbal teas and for culinary purposes.

Cosmetic Benefits

Improves skin health, used in skincare products.
