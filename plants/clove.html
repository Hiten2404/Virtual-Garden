Neem (Azadirachta indica)

Description

The Neem tree is known for its immense medicinal properties. It is evergreen and native to the Indian subcontinent. Every part of the tree is used in Ayurvedic medicine.

Medicinal Uses

  • Acts as a natural blood purifier
  • Used in treating skin infections, acne, and wounds
  • Known for its anti-inflammatory and antifungal properties
  • Used in dental care and oral hygiene

Care Tips

  • Thrives in full sun and well-drained soil
  • Water moderately, avoid overwatering
  • Can be grown in pots or directly in soil
