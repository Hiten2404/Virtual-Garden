Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)

Ashwagandha, also known as Indian ginseng, is a powerful herb used in Ayurveda for its rejuvenating and stress-relieving properties.
Plant Attributes & Uses

Scientific Classification

Family: Solanaceae
Genus: Withania
Species: W. somnifera

Global Spread

Native to India, also grown in the Middle East, Africa, and China.

Preferred Climate

Warm, dry climates, thrives in well-drained soil.

Medicinal Uses

Reduces stress, boosts immunity, supports sleep, enhances vitality.

Traditional Uses

Used in Ayurveda for strengthening, rejuvenation, and as a general tonic.

Ayurvedic Benefits

Balances Vata and Kapha, calms the nervous system, improves energy.
