Tulsi (Ocimum sanctum)

Tulsi, also known as Holy Basil, is a sacred plant in Ayurveda known for its medicinal and spiritual significance.
Plant Attributes & Uses

Scientific Classification

Family: Lamiaceae
Genus: Ocimum
Species: O. sanctum

Global Spread

Native to India, also found in Southeast Asia, and grown worldwide.

Preferred Climate

Tropical and subtropical climates, thrives in well-drained soil.

Medicinal Uses

Boosts immunity, reduces stress, supports respiratory health, and acts as an adaptogen.

Traditional Uses

Used in Ayurveda for purification, respiratory support, and spiritual rituals.

Ayurvedic Benefits

Balances Kapha and Vata, promotes mental clarity, enhances digestion.
