Lavender (Lavandula angustifolia)

Lavender is a fragrant flowering plant prized for its calming aroma and beautiful purple blooms. Used in herbal medicine, cosmetics, and culinary applications.
Plant Attributes & Uses

Scientific Classification

Family: Lamiaceae
Genus: Lavandula
Species: L. angustifolia

Global Spread

Widely cultivated in temperate climates around the world, especially in Mediterranean regions.

Preferred Climate

Thrives in full sun and dry, well-drained soil. Prefers mild winters and hot summers.

Medicinal Uses

Reduces anxiety, aids sleep, soothes headaches, and supports skin health.

Traditional Uses

Used in sachets, teas, and essential oils for relaxation and aromatic purposes.

Cosmetic Benefits

Used in perfumes, lotions, soaps; known for antibacterial and calming properties.
