Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera)

The Lotus is an aquatic perennial plant revered in many cultures for its beauty, symbolism, and medicinal qualities. It thrives in shallow ponds and wetlands, emerging with majestic blooms from muddy waters.
Plant Attributes & Uses

Scientific Classification

Family: Nelumbonaceae
Genus: Nelumbo
Species: N. nucifera

Global Spread

Native to Asia and Northern Australia; grown in ornamental and sacred ponds worldwide.

Preferred Climate

Tropical to subtropical climates with warm temperatures and abundant water.

Medicinal Uses

Lotus leaves, flowers, and seeds are used in traditional medicine for calming, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant benefits.

Traditional Uses

Symbolizes purity and rebirth; seeds and roots used in cuisine and rituals.

Cosmetic Benefits

Extracts used for anti-aging, hydration, and soothing skincare formulations.
