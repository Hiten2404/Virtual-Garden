Neem (Azadirachta indica)

Neem is a fast-growing tree native to India, known for its remarkable medicinal properties. Every part of the tree is used in traditional medicine, making it one of the most valued plants in Ayurveda.
Plant Attributes & Uses

Scientific Classification

Family: Meliaceae
Genus: Azadirachta
Species: A. indica

Global Spread

Cultivated in India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Africa.

Preferred Climate

Tropical and subtropical regions, prefers full sun.

Medicinal Uses

Anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, antifungal, and antiviral properties.

Traditional Uses

Treats skin conditions, dental care, boosts immunity.

Ayurvedic Benefits

Balances pitta and kapha, detoxifies the body, enhances skin health.
