Moringa (Moringa oleifera)

Moringa, also known as the drumstick tree or miracle tree, is a highly nutritious plant renowned for its edible leaves, pods, and seeds. It's valued in herbal medicine and nutrition around the world.
Plant Attributes & Uses

Scientific Classification

Family: Moringaceae
Genus: Moringa
Species: M. oleifera

Global Spread

Native to the Indian subcontinent, now grown throughout Africa, Asia, and South America.

Preferred Climate

Warm, tropical and subtropical climates with low to moderate rainfall.

Medicinal Uses

Rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals; supports immunity, reduces inflammation, and balances blood sugar.

Traditional Uses

Leaves eaten fresh, dried, or powdered; used in soups, teas, and health supplements.

Cosmetic Benefits

Moringa oil used in skincare for its moisturizing and rejuvenating effects.
