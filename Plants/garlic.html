Garlic (Allium sativum)

Garlic is a widely used herb known for its strong aroma and medicinal properties. It has been used for centuries in cooking, medicine, and traditional practices.
Plant Attributes & Uses

Scientific Classification

Family: Amaryllidaceae
Genus: Allium
Species: A. sativum

Global Spread

Grown worldwide in various climates.

Preferred Climate

Cool, sunny locations with well-drained soil.

Medicinal Uses

Antibacterial, antifungal, boosts immunity, supports heart health.

Traditional Uses

Used in cooking, natural remedies, and as a pest repellent.

Culinary Benefits

Enhances flavor in dishes, used in spices, oils, and sauces.
